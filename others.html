KittenBot

Discord Bot Pet

KittenBot is a simple discord bot, where it acts as a pet that will require it to be taken care of. There are 3 main stats that users will need to monitor; health, hunger and happiness. As time goes on, these stats will slowly drop, and it is up to members of the server to feed, play and care for it.
